Wythoff's Game
(Generalized)
How to Play
• Play against the computer. You and the computer take turns making moves.

• At the start of the game, there are at least two piles of counters.

• On your turn, choose at least one pile. Take the same number of counters from each of the piles you chose.

• Whoever takes the last counter wins.
